DESIGN DIRECTION
Problem with current design: Oversized retro-looking numbers, plain text, no visual containers, flat dotted background with zero depth or hierarchy.
New design direction: Premium SaaS process section. Think Linear, Vercel, Notion process pages. Each step is a self-contained card with a clean number badge, icon, title, and body. Cards sit on the dark navy background with a subtle surface elevation. A connecting line runs between steps on desktop. The whole section feels structured, modern, and trustworthy.
Aesthetic: Clean dark-surface cards with thin glowing borders. Step numbers are small elegant badges — NOT giant decorative text. Connecting line between cards creates a visual flow. Framer Motion stagger animation reveals each card as it scrolls into view.
